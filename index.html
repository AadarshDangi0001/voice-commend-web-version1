Accessibility Challenges

Many users with mobility impairments or disabilities struggle to navigate websites using traditional mouse and keyboard controls. Our voice command extension enables hands-free browsing, making the web more inclusive and accessible.

Productivity Limitations

Multitasking while browsing can be inefficient, especially for professionals who need to work hands-free. Our extension allows users to control their browser using voice commands, improving workflow and efficiency.

User Fatigue & Convenience

Constantly clicking, scrolling, and typing can cause strain and fatigue. With voice commands, users can perform actions effortlessly, reducing physical strain and enhancing overall browsing convenience.
